TASK RULES

Participants will have to make soccer playing robot to take part in this event. The team with highest score will be declared as the winner. .

Team has to be present at the arena 10 minutes before game starts. Absence will result in direct disqualification of the team.

One person cannot be part of two teams.

Only one person from the team can control the robot at one point of time.

The robots shall be inspected before the event. Failing the inspection shall lead to disqualification.

The organizers reserve the right to change the rules as they deem fit.

Practice arena will not be provided to the participants before the event starts.

Both the teams can request for one time out of no more than 2 min in duration, and for every extra time out there will be penalty of 20 points for every 2 min.

Judges decision will be final.

The maximum alloed dimensions of the robot are 30cmx30cm.

Maximum weight allowed is 5 kg.(Max 10% tolerance is allowed)
